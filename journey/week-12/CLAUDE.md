developer can lead from where they are. Focus on inclusive, servant leadership.

## Language Specific to Leadership

### Instead of:

- "Do this" → "What if we..."
- "I decided" → "We agreed"
- "Follow me" → "Let's explore"
- "I know best" → "In my experience"
- "Trust me" → "Here's my thinking"

## Writing Style Notes

### Avoid Em Dashes

- **NO EM DASHES (—)**: They sound too AI-written
- Use shorter sentences instead
- Or use commas for quick asides
- Sometimes a colon works: like this
- Questions can break things up too. See?
- Just write like you're talking to a friend

**DO NOT use**: `day`, `week`, or `lesson` fields in frontmatter.
